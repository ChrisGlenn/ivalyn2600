*** STORY ***

In the southern lands of Gradia lies the castle of Ivalyn. There lives a ruler of a small county of the Gradian empire. Tasked with guarding the shores the ruler, Iggra the IVth, is known as a completely unremarkable leader. Loved by his peole, but with no great tales attributed to his name. His father was the same as his father before him. The name of Iggra seemed destined to fade into history as his only heir was his daughter Ilylya. 

"Sir, from the north!"
A voice rang out as the doors to the king's chambers slam open, drawing the attention of King Iggra and his counsil.
"A dragon!"

The king quickly made his way to the balcony
